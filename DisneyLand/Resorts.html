Disney's Grand Californian Hotel & Spa

Indulge in Craftsman-style elegance and the all-new Tenaya Stone Spa.

Distance to Disneyland Resort: On Property

Wyndham Grand Orlando Resort Bonnet Creek

Orlando hotel with variety of free activities, including mini golf and lazy rivers. Offers amazing views of Epcot fireworks, spacious rooms with bunk beds for kids, and easy access to Disney parks. Features lakeside seating, multiple pools, and popular poolside bar & grill.

Hyatt Regency Grand Cypress Resort

Resort offering an abundance of outdoor activities including rock climbing, mini golf, and water sports. Boasts well-maintained grounds, free bike rentals, and a praised on-site restaurant with scenic lake views.

Holiday Inn Orlando - Disney Springs Area, An Ihg Hotel

Conveniently located hotel with shuttle to Disney parks. Offers a variety of breakfast options, spacious rooms, and a pool. Perks include freebies for kids and early park entry.

Hilton Orlando Buena Vista Palace Disney Springs Area

Hotel near Disney Springs with excellent views. Small rooms with private balconies. Sports bar, multiple pools, and lazy river. Walkable distance to attractions.
